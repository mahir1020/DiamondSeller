Welcome to Shriya Gems

Founded by Mr. Samir Patel, a diamond industry expert with over 20 years of experience, Shriya Gems is committed to offering the finest natural and lab-grown diamonds backed by authenticity, ethics, and excellence. Based in Surat, Gujarat — the global hub of diamond craftsmanship — we bring generations of expertise and cutting-edge innovation to every sparkle we deliver.

What We Do

  • IGI/GIA-certified natural and lab-grown diamonds
  • Custom diamond jewellery designed for engagement, weddings, and timeless gifts
  • Ethical sourcing and transparent pricing
  • Wholesale and retail solutions for individual buyers and businesses

Each diamond is handpicked and graded with precision, ensuring unmatched brilliance and value. Whether you’re looking for a classic solitaire, a custom-designed ring, or a loose diamond for investment — we offer a wide, curated selection with personal guidance at every step.

Our Legacy

What sets us apart is our founder’s legacy. With two decades of hands-on experience in the diamond sector, Mr. Samir Patel has built a brand that values integrity over gimmicks and craftsmanship over compromise. His leadership ensures that every Shriya Gems customer receives honest advice, quality assurance, and a truly memorable buying experience.

Our Vision

We aim to empower modern customers with access to both earth-mined and eco-conscious lab-grown diamonds, helping them make informed choices that reflect both personal and planetary values.
